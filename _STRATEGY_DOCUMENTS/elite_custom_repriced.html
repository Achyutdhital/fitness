Elite and Custom Coach repriced with correct trainer pay and platform margin

Fixing Elite pricing so trainer pay, AI/infra costs, and platform margin all fit cleanly.

Why $59.99 Elite doesn't work

Old $59.99 breakdown Trainer: 1 hr session pay ~$25–35 · AI + infra: ~$8 · Total cost: ~$43 · Platform left: ~$17 · That's only 28% margin — and barely covers support, payment fees, video infra
The fix Price Elite so that after trainer session pay + AI + infra, platform keeps a healthy ~30–35% margin. 2 sessions of 30 min = 1 hour trainer time. Trainer hourly rate varies by tier. Price must reflect that fully.

Elite chat access rule

Not unlimited chat Trainer gets overwhelmed, burns out, quality drops. Unlimited chat is unsustainable at any price point unless you charge separately.
Fixed message quota Elite gets 10 trainer messages/week (questions, check-ins). Trainer must respond within 48 hrs. Extra messages = locked until next week. Clean, fair, manageable.
